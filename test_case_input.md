**Proposed Mechanic: "Abyssal VIP Portal"**

**Overview:** 
We are introducing a new limited-time event called the "Abyssal VIP Portal" to drive Q3 revenue. Players can purchase a premium, time-limited currency called "Void Crystals" using real money. 

**Mechanics:**
1. **The Portal (Gacha):** Players use Void Crystals to open the Abyssal Portal to obtain random character shards. The portal has a "pity system" that guarantees a high-tier shard after 100 pulls, but we don't plan to show the exact drop rates for individual pulls in the UI to keep the mystery alive. 
2. **VIP Subscription Tiers:** Players can subscribe to a "Void Lord VIP" monthly pass. This pass automatically deducts from the user's account and provides daily Void Crystals. If a player tries to cancel, they have to navigate through 4 different confirmation screens detailing what they will lose. If they do cancel, they lose all accumulated VIP points instantly.
3. **Time-Limited Currency Expiry:** Any Void Crystals purchased during the event will automatically expire and disappear from the player's inventory 24 hours after the event ends, creating a strong sense of urgency to spend them quickly.
4. **Target Audience:** The game is rated 9+ and features cute, cartoon-style characters, though we don't explicitly target children.
